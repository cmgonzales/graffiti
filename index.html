About me

Born in New York city. Dani believes art gives mankind an essential and irreplaceable cultural language in which to express and know itself. Dani believes art can transcend cultural and socio-economic barriers, fostering understanding, mutual respect and unity among disparate peoples.

Streets of New York

The street was glorious in its inception. The sidewalks were smooth grey stones, joined with such precision that the joins were almost invisible. The walls were concrete, but not like a villa in rural Spain; they were more akin to the construction of a modernist skyscraper, all sharp edges and corners.

The inner City

The inner city grew out of the cracked sidewalk like the jagged gap-toothed grin of an old junkie. The only splash of color in the grime came from the lurid graffiti and the sidewalks were littered with injection paraphernalia
